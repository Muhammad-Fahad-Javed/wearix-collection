Our Journey

The Story Behind WEARIX

WEARIX COLLECTION was born from a simple observation: finding high-quality, well-fitting oversized T-shirts in Pakistan shouldn't be difficult.

We started with a clear mission — to create clothing that combines premium materials, modern design, and accessible pricing. Every piece is designed with care, tested for comfort, and crafted to become a staple in your wardrobe.

Our name, WEARIX, reflects what we stand for: confidence in what you wear, quality you can feel, and style that speaks for itself.

"Confidence Begins with WEARIX" — it's not just our tagline, it's our promise.

Craftsmanship

Materials & Craftsmanship

Every WEARIX garment starts with 100% combed ring-spun cotton — a premium fabric that's softer, stronger, and more breathable than standard cotton.

We work with trusted manufacturing partners in Pakistan who share our commitment to quality and ethical production. Each piece is cut, sewn, and finished with care, ensuring consistent fit and durability across every size and color.

Our fabrics are pre-washed to minimize shrinkage and tested for colorfastness so your WEARIX pieces stay looking great, wash after wash.

Quality

Manufacturing & Quality Standards

Our manufacturing process is built around a simple principle: quality at every step.

From fabric selection to cutting, sewing, and finishing, each WEARIX piece undergoes multiple quality checks. We inspect for stitching consistency, fabric integrity, color accuracy, and fit precision before any garment leaves our facility.

We also work with our manufacturing partners to ensure ethical labor practices, safe working conditions, and environmental responsibility. We believe great clothing should be made with care — for the people who wear it and the people who make it.

Sustainability & Ethical Practices Optional

Doing It Responsibly

At WEARIX, we're committed to reducing our environmental footprint and building a brand that's as responsible as it is stylish.

We use 100% cotton — a natural, biodegradable fiber — and we're exploring more sustainable packaging options. We believe in making quality clothing that lasts, reducing the need for fast fashion replacements.

We're also proud to manufacture locally in Pakistan, supporting our local economy and reducing transportation emissions.

We're on a journey to do better — and we're committed to sharing our progress transparently.

Shipping Information

Nationwide Delivery Across Pakistan

We ship to every major city and town in Pakistan, including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar, Quetta, and more.

Policy

Exchange & Return Policy

7-Day Exchange Policy

We want you to love your WEARIX purchase. If you need a different size or have any concerns, we're here to make it right.

  • Eligibility: Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached
  • Timeframe: Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order
  • Process: Reach out via WhatsApp or email with your order details and the size you need
  • Shipping: We'll arrange the exchange at no extra cost

We don't offer cash refunds at this time, but we'll replace your item with the correct size or a different style of equal value.
